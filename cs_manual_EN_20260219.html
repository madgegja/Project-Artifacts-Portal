2.1 How to Cancel — Self-Service Instructions

Template 2.1.1: Cancel via Website

Hello, this is Toomics.
Thank you for contacting us.

It looks like you wish to cancel your VIP Membership.

You can easily cancel your subscription directly from your account:

How to Cancel on Website:

  1. Go to toomics.com and log in
  2. Click on your profile icon (top right)
  3. Go to My Page → Subscription Info
  4. Click Cancel Subscription
  5. Confirm cancellation

After cancellation, you will still have access to VIP content until the end of your current billing period.

If you need further assistance, please feel free to contact us.

Best regards,
Toomics

Template 2.1.2: Cancel via Google Play Store

Hello, this is Toomics.
Thank you for contacting us.

Since your subscription was purchased through the Google Play Store, it must be canceled through Google Play:

How to Cancel on Google Play:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app
  2. Tap your profile icon (top right)
  3. Tap Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  4. Find "Toomics" and tap it
  5. Tap Cancel subscription
  6. Follow the on-screen instructions

Please note that canceling through Toomics website alone does NOT stop Google Play billing. You must cancel directly through Google Play.

If you have any further questions, please feel free to contact us.

Best regards,
Toomics

Template 2.1.3: Cancel via Apple App Store (iOS)

Hello, this is Toomics.
Thank you for contacting us.

Since your subscription was purchased through the Apple App Store, it must be canceled through Apple:

How to Cancel on iPhone/iPad:

  1. Open Settings on your device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find "Toomics" and tap it
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Important: We are unable to cancel Apple subscriptions on our end. This must be done directly through your Apple device or Apple ID settings.

If you have any further questions, please feel free to contact us.

Best regards,
Toomics

Template 2.1.4: Cancel via PayPal

Hello, this is Toomics.
Thank you for contacting us.

To cancel your PayPal recurring payment:

How to Cancel on PayPal:

  1. Log in to your PayPal account
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Click Payments → Manage automatic payments
  4. Find "TOOMIC" or "Toomics" and click it
  5. Click Cancel → Confirm

Alternatively, we can process the cancellation on our end. Please confirm if you would like us to do so.

11.2 Quality Checklist

Before sending any response, verify:

  • Correct greeting in the customer's language
  • Customer's name/email verified (if applicable)
  • Account information confirmed in our system
  • Clear action taken (cancellation, refund, etc.) with specific dates
  • Any remaining VIP access period mentioned
  • No further charges confirmation (when applicable)
  • Professional closing in the correct language
  • No personal information exposed (PII)
  • Template customized for the specific situation (not generic copy-paste)

11.3 Escalation Criteria

Escalate to Team Lead when:

  • Customer threatens legal action (especially DE market)
  • Multiple chargebacks from same account
  • Suspected fraud requiring investigation
  • Payment discrepancy > $100 USD
  • Customer complaint about agent behavior
  • Media/influencer complaint
  • Requests involving 3+ accounts

Also escalate to Team Lead when:

  • Chargeback dispute requiring evidence submission
  • Platform-wide technical issues affecting multiple users
  • New fraud patterns not covered by existing guidelines
  • Policy exceptions needed (e.g., refund beyond 14-day window)
